Strengths-Quest Assessment Results


Communication

As an effective communicator, I am able to organize my thoughts and ideas while effectively presenting or communicating them to my students or colleagues.

Developer

As a developer, I see the potential in others. Very often, in fact, potential is all I see. When interacting with others, my goal is to help them experience success - I look for ways to challenge them.

Activator

As an activator, I naturally initiate turning thoughts into action. I believe that action is the best device for learning; once a decision is made and action is taken, I evaluate the result and I learn.

Individualization

With a top strength in individualization, I am intrigued with the unique qualities of each person and I have a strong ability to figure out how people who are different can work together productively.

Adaptability

I have a strong ability to live in the present - meaning that I can change, and change quickly if need be. Rather than being rigid, I am flexible; and rather than being tied to a pre-set plan, I can quickly change to meet immediate demands and circumstances that require attention.
